Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

A comprehensive program in finance, accounting, taxation, and business management.

Course Overview

The B.Com program at Vidya Sagar Institute is designed to provide a strong foundation in commerce and business. Students gain practical and theoretical exposure in areas like accounting, economics, business law, finance, taxation, marketing, and management.

  • Full 3-year undergraduate program
  • Industry-oriented curriculum
  • Expert commerce faculty
  • Internship & practical exposure
  • Career-oriented training & placement assistance

Key Subjects

Financial Accounting

Understanding balance sheets, P&L, and accounting principles.

Business Economics

Micro & macro economic analysis for decision-making.

Corporate Law

Legal structure and regulations of companies.

Taxation

Income tax, GST, and tax planning.

Management Accounting

Costing, budgeting, and managerial decision-making.

Marketing Management

Customer behavior, branding, and marketing strategy.

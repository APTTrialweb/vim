Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A modern program designed to build strong business, leadership, and management skills.

Course Overview

The BBA program at Vidya Sagar Institute focuses on essential business skills including marketing, finance, HR, operations, communication, and entrepreneurship. The course integrates theory with real-world applications to prepare students for corporate roles or further postgraduate studies like MBA.

  • 3-year full-time undergraduate program
  • Case studies, presentations & live projects
  • Business communication & personality development
  • Industry visits & guest lectures
  • Internship opportunities & placement support

Core Subjects

Principles of Management

Foundation of business operations & leadership techniques.

Business Mathematics

Quantitative techniques used for business decisions.

Marketing Management

Branding, advertising, and consumer behaviour.

Financial Accounting

Understanding financial statements & basic accounting.

Human Resource Management

Recruitment, training, performance & employee relations.

Business Law

Legal aspects of business & contracts.
